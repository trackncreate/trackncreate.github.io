How to download?

Steps:

  1. Go to download section through the above button or using menu
  2. Search and find the project from the list or search bar
  3. you will find the project details, extensions, files etc. scroll down and click download
  4. You will be directed to a download page (pop-ads may be visible when you click download, you can close it and continue download), download .aia, .apk, assets etc
  5. That's it project Ready to use!
