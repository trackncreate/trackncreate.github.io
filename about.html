About This Site

This website is connected to the Youtube channel track n create, and is dedicated to teaching MIT App Inventor through tutorials, sample projects, and downloadable files. The goal is to help beginners and enthusiasts create Android apps easily and effectively.

About Me

I am Sachin Thankachan, an educational content creator focusing on MIT App Inventor tutorials. I share step-by-step videos on my YouTube channel and provide project files here to help learners follow along and practice.
